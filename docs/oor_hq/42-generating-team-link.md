# Generating a Project Team Link

A team link is a shareable URL that multiple people can use to join your project. Unlike email invites, a team link can be used by anyone who has it (up to the expiry time).

---

## Who Can Generate a Team Link

Only users with the **Admin** role on the project, or company **Admins**, can generate or regenerate team links.

---

## How to Generate a Team Link

1. Open the project you want to share
2. Click the **Share** button
3. Select the **Team Link** tab
4. Choose the expiry duration (e.g. 24 hours or 48 hours)
5. Click **Generate Link**
6. Copy the link and share it (e.g. via Slack, email, or chat)

---

## How Team Links Work

- Anyone with the link who is logged in can accept it and join the project
- The role assigned to all link acceptors is set when you generate the link
- The link can be used multiple times until it expires
- Once expired, the link stops working — you must generate a new one

---

## Regenerating a Link

Clicking **Regenerate Link** revokes the previous link and creates a new one. Anyone who has the old link will no longer be able to use it. People who already accepted the old link keep their project access.

---

## When to Use a Team Link vs Email Invite

| Situation | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| Inviting one specific person | Email invite |
| Onboarding a small group at once (e.g. a sprint team) | Team link |
| You want to control who can join (single-use guarantee) | Email invite |
| Quick access for a short window | Team link with 24h expiry |
