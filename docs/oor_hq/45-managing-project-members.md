# Managing Project Members

Project admins can view current members, revoke pending invites, and remove existing members from the Share panel.

---

## Who Can Manage Members

Users with the **Admin** role on the project, or company **Admins**.

---

## Viewing Current Members

1. Open the project
2. Click the **Share** button
3. Select the **Members** tab
4. The list shows all current members along with their role and when they joined

Pending (not yet accepted) email invites are also listed here, showing the invited email, role, and expiry date.

---

## Revoking a Pending Invite

If someone has not yet accepted their invite and you want to cancel it:

1. In the **Members** tab, find the pending invite
2. Click **Revoke**
3. The invite link is immediately invalidated — even if the person clicks the link after this, it will not work

---

## Removing a Member

To remove someone who has already accepted an invite:

1. In the **Members** tab, find the member
2. Click **Remove**
3. Confirm the removal

The member loses access to the project and all its boards, mindmaps, and notes immediately. Any board-level memberships they had inside this project are also removed.

---

## Changing a Member's Role

There is no direct "change role" action. To change someone's role:

1. Remove the existing member
2. Send them a new invite with the correct role
