# Sending a Project Email Invite

Email invites let you invite a specific person to your project. The invite is single-use and expires after 7 days.

---

## Who Can Send Invites

Only users with the **Admin** role on the project, or company **Admins**, can send project invites.

---

## How to Send an Email Invite

1. Open the project you want to share
2. Click the **Share** button (top-right of the project page)
3. The Share panel opens — select the **Email** tab
4. Enter the invitee's email address
5. Choose their role: **Viewer**, **Collaborator**, or **Admin**
6. Click **Send Invite**

The invitee will receive an email with a link to accept the invitation.

---

## What the Invitee Receives

The invitee gets an email with a unique acceptance link. When they click it:

- If they already have an account, they log in and are added to the project immediately
- If they do not have an account, they are prompted to register first

The invite link expires after **7 days**. After that, the link shows a "This invite has expired" page. You would need to send a new invite.

---

## In-App Notification for Existing Company Members

If the email address belongs to someone who is already a member of your company, they also receive an in-app notification (in the notification bell) right away — they do not need to wait for the email.

This in-app notification is only sent if the user has the `project_invite` notification type enabled in their notification preferences.

---

## Invite Limits and Expiry

- Each email invite link can only be used **once**
- The invite expires after **7 days** from when it was sent
- A single person can only have one active invite per project at a time
- You can see pending invites in the **Members** tab of the Share panel and revoke them if needed
