# Accepting a Project Invite

When someone shares a project with you, you receive an invite link. This guide explains how to accept it.

---

## Accepting an Email Invite

1. Open the invite email and click the acceptance link
2. If you are not logged in, you will be redirected to the login page — log in (or register if you do not have an account yet)
3. After logging in, you are taken to the invite acceptance page showing the project name, your assigned role, and the expiry date
4. Click **Accept Invite**
5. You are redirected to the project immediately

---

## Accepting a Team Link

Team links work the same way:

1. Click or paste the link into your browser
2. Log in if you are not already
3. Review the invite details and click **Accept**
4. You are added to the project and redirected

---

## What Happens After Accepting

- You are added as a **ProjectMember** with the role the inviter chose
- The project appears in your **Projects** page under the **Shared with me** tab
- You can access all boards, mindmaps, and notes in the project according to your role

---

## Common Issues

**"This invite has expired"** — The invite link is older than 7 days (email) or past its team link expiry. Ask the project admin to send a new invite.

**"This invite is no longer valid"** — The invite was revoked by the project admin. Ask them to send a new one.

**"You already have access to this project"** — You have already accepted a previous invite. You can navigate to the project directly from your Projects page.

---

## Which Account to Use

You can accept a project invite regardless of which company account you are logged into. The project access is linked to your user account, not your company session. Once accepted, you can access the project by switching to any company context and looking at the **Shared with me** tab.
